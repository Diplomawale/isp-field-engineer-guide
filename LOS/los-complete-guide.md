# LOS (Line of Sight) – Complete Guide

## 📌 What is LOS?

LOS means **Line of Sight**.

It refers to a **clear path between two antennas**.

---

## 📡 Why LOS is Important?

- Ensures strong signal
- Prevents signal loss
- Maintains stable connection

---

## 🚧 Common Obstacles

- Buildings
- Trees
- Towers
- Hills

---

## 🛠 How to Check LOS in Field

1. Identify both sites
2. Measure distance
3. Check obstacles
4. Adjust antenna height
5. Use tools like Google Earth

---

## 📊 Tools Used

- Google Earth
- Ubiquiti AirLink
- GPS Apps
- Compass

---

## ⚠️ Without LOS

- Weak signal
- Link instability
- Frequent disconnection

---

## 📡 Field Tip

Even small obstacles can affect signal quality in long-distance links.
